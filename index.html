About RelationMap

Created by HappyGuy119

This tool helps visualize complex human relationships. Start by tapping the “Add Person” button or double-clicking anywhere on the canvas to create a person. Use the Grouping feature to categorize individuals into communities or organizations to see where different worlds overlap or remain isolated.

Turn on link mode and tap two persons to create a connection.

Connection Styles:

  • Dotted: Frequent interaction or mutual recognition.
  • Solid: Close collaborators, family, or high-trust partnerships.
  • Dashed: Almost no interaction or "neighbor-only" awareness.

Of course, you can use this mind map-making tool for other purposes. While designed for people, this logic works perfectly for mapping project dependencies, software architectures, or historical events.

Your map is stored exclusively in your browser’s cache. It persists when you close the window, but clearing your cache will delete it. To save your work permanently, use the Export to JSON feature to save a file to your device.
